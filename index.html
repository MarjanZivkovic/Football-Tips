English

Display:

Upon opening the app you'll get football matches for the current day listed on the screen. Only the games that hadn't been started yet or are in play will be listed. You can filter the games by league or country via the search field.

By clicking on the Our Tips button, you'll get our betting tips for the given match:

Possible outcomes:

In the Score field you can get next tips:

  • 1 Home team wins the game
  • X Score is a tie
  • 2 Away team wins the game

In the Goals field you can get next tips:

  • 3+ Sum of goals is 3 or more
  • 0-2 Sum of goals is 2 or less

In both fields, you can get the " - " sign (most often). This means that the app is unable to predict the outcome of the given match. The reason for that can be the lack of previous matches between the two teams or the unpredictability of those matches.

The logic:

The app predicts the outcomes of matches primarily based on previous matches between the teams ( Head to Head ). For serious bets, our advice is to also take into account the current form of teams, positions on the table, number of injured players... Use our tips as an addition to your betting arsenal.

Important!

Even though we did our best to provide you with solid suggestions, our tips can be wrong due to myriad of different factors that can affect a game of football. Therefore, we won't assume any responsibility for the wrong tips!

We've put a lot of effort to help you bet more efficiently. We'll continue working on improving this app. Let us know if you were successful, nothing makes us happier. Good luck!

Srpski

Prikaz:

Po otvaranju aplikacije na ekranu ćete dobiti sve fudbalske mečeve za tekući dan. Samo utakmice koje još uvek nisu počele ili su u toku će biti izlistane. Možete filtrirati mečeve po ligi ili državi u polju za pretragu.

Klikom na dugme Naši Tipovi (Our Tips), dobićete naše predloge za klađenje za dati meč.

Mogući ishodi:

U polju za rezultat ( Score ) možete dobiti sledeće tipove:

  • 1 Domaćin pobeđuje
  • X Nerešen rezultat
  • 2 Gost pobeđuje

U polju za golove ( Goals ) možete dobiti sledeće tipove:

  • 3+ Zbir golova je 3 ili više
  • 0-2 Zbir golova je 2 ili manje

U oba polja možete dobiti " - " znak (najčešće). Ovo znači da aplikacija nije u mogućnosti da predvidi ishod meča. Razlog za to može biti nedovoljno prethodnih susreta između dva tima ili nedovoljna predvidivost tih susreta.

Logika:

Aplikacija predviđa ishode mečeva primarno na bazi prethodnih susreta timova ( Head to Head ). Za ozbiljno klađenje, naš savet je da takođe uzmete u obzir trenutnu formu timova, pozicije na tabeli, broj povređenih igrača... Koristite naše tipove kao dodatak Vašem kladioničarskom arsenalu.
